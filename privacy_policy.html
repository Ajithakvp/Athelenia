Privacy Policy

Describes how Aadukalam Pvt Ltd (“Company”, “We”, “Us”, or “Our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use our sports performance and academy management platform, Athelenia, accessible through our mobile application and website.

By using Athelenia, you agree to the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Definitions

Company refers to Aadukalam Pvt Ltd, the owner and operator of Athelenia.

Platform refers to the Athelenia mobile app, website, and related services.

Academy means a registered sports training institution using Athelenia to manage athletes, coaches, and operational data.

Device means any device used to access the Platform such as a smartphone, tablet, or computer.

Personal Data refers to information that identifies or relates to an identifiable individual.

Service refers to Athelenia’s features, including performance tracking, analytics, communication tools, and academy operations management.

Service Provider means any third-party entity engaged by the Company to support delivery or enhancement of the Platform.

Usage Data refers to data collected automatically from your device when using the Platform.

User means any individual or organization accessing Athelenia, including:

Athletes receiving coaching, performance feedback, and training insights.

Coaches managing athletes, providing feedback, and monitoring progress.

Academy Owners/Admins handling academy-level operations like attendance, payments, and performance tracking.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

A ) Personal Data

✦ Name, contact information, age, gender

✦ Photos, training footage, and performance records

✦ Role-specific information (e.g., athlete stats, coach feedback)

B ) Usage Data

✦ Device details, operating system, IP address

✦ App interaction data, page views, and navigation behavior

✦ Crash reports and diagnostic logs

C ) Academy Data

✦ Attendance records

✦ Payment transactions and schedules

✦ Feedback logs, home workout data, and coach reports

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

✦ Deliver performance insights, training tasks, and feedback

✦ Manage academy operations including attendance and payroll

✦ Facilitate communication between athletes, coaches, and admins

✦ Improve Platform features, user experience, and system security

✦ Fulfill legal obligations and service agreements
