ABOUT OUR PLATFORM

Athelenia is an academy-centric performance analytics and management platform. Our features include:

✦ Athlete performance tracking & feedback

✦ Attendance management

✦ Coach payments & scheduling

✦ Personalized home workouts

✦ Academy-wise performance reports

✦ Role-based access for athletes, coaches, and academy owners

We operate academy-wise, meaning each account is linked to a specific sports academy. Athletes and coaches are managed under that academy structure

USER ROLES

You may use the Platform in one or more of the following roles:

Athlete : You will receive performance insights, training tasks, and track progress.

Coach : You can view athlete performance, provide feedback, and manage training plans.

Owner/Admin: You can manage academies, track attendance, handle payments, and oversee staff and athlete activities.

Each role has distinct access rights and responsibilities.
